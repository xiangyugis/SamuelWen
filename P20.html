Designing a 15 mile trail race

A challenging but gorgeous trail.

The trail race course is located in the south of Ellensburg, WA. The starting point and endpoint of this race trail are at the intersection of Manastash road and Umptanum road. The total length of the race trail is 15.5 miles. About 7 miles of the race trail is on public roads, and 8 miles of it stays on the Manastash Ridge Trail.
